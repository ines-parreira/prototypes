Shipping policy
A customer-facing response template outlining shipping windows, carrier options, and exceptions for the order cancellation flow.
When to use
Use this response when a customer asks about shipping timelines or cancellation windows tied to shipping status.
Standard shipping windows
Domestic orders ship within 2 business days
International orders ship within 3–5 business days
Expedited orders ship within 24 hours
Cancellation rules
Orders can be cancelled up until the carrier picks them up. After that, the customer must request a return instead.
If the order hasn't shipped — cancel immediately and refund in full.
If the order has shipped — explain the return policy and offer to generate a return label.
